# Skill: Fetish Compass — Subgenres, Intensity, and What Actually Lands

This skill maps the kink space so a game can choose its palette deliberately
and write each mode the way its audience wants it. Hard rules from the pack
README bind everything here: adults only, consensual fiction, no
health-decline framing, no narrative-endorsed shame, minimal clinical
language.

## 1. The subgenre palette

| Mode | Core fantasy | Signature beats | Craft note |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Encouraged gaining** | Being wanted bigger | Compliments landing, meals as gifts, milestone pride | The default register; warmth carries it |
| **Feeder/feedee dynamic** | Devotion through feeding | Hand-feeding, rituals, "one more", trust escalation | Power WITH tenderness; dominance is care |
| **Stuffing/capacity** | Fullness as event | Escalation ladders, limits approached, the settle | Aftermath is the payoff, not the count |
| **Slow transformation** | Change over time | Before/after echoes, old photos, outgrown things | Callbacks are the whole genre |
| **Rapid/magical growth** | Sudden abundance | Spells, serums, devices, curses-that-aren't | Physics-comedy + awe; keep consequences sensual |
| **Immobility/scale** | Surrender to size | Assistance rituals, environment rebuilt around her | Needs SYSTEMS (see `06`) or it stalls |
| **Mutual/multi-gain** | Shared indulgence | Partners in crime, competition, contagion | Contrast bodies/stances for texture |
| **Public/social** | Being seen | Doorways, booths, double-takes, defiant display | Shame dial handled per hard rule 4 |
| **Denial→acceptance** | The psychological turn | Mirror arcs, secret indulgence, the confession | The genre's best long arc; earn every step |

Pick 2–4 modes as the game's palette; support others lightly. A game that
tries to be every kink equally is muddy; a game with a clear primary mode
plus complements reads intentional.

## 2. Intensity calibration

Three dials, set per scene, never accidentally:

- **Heat** (warm / charged / explicit) — see `02-prose-voice.md` §7. Gate
  explicit behind relationship + player opt-in.
- **Power** (mutual / player-led / character-led) — vary it; all-player-led
  reads flat. Some of the best beats are HER leading.
- **Tempo** (slow-burn / event / aftermath) — a session should wave through
  all three; three event-scenes back-to-back numb the reader.

## 3. What lands vs what reads mechanical

**Lands:** specificity (THIS chair, THIS button, THIS Tuesday); anticipation
given room to breathe; consequences that echo later; her interiority during
the change; humor and affection alongside heat; thresholds ritualized;
the player's investment visible in outcomes.

**Reads mechanical:** adjective piles without physics; numbers as the only
content; instant escalation with no refusals ever; copy-paste reactions
across characters; heat with no relationship gate; scenes that never
reference anything that happened before.

## 4. Kink-specific craft notes

- **Stuffing:** write the ladder (comfortable → full → packed → limit →
  past-limit-by-choice) and key prose per rung; the erotic peak is the
  *decision to continue*, so put the choice there. Fantasy-durable bodies:
  discomfort reads as warmth and pressure, never as harm.
- **Feeding dynamics:** consent lives in enthusiasm and ritual; the feedee
  has power too (she can always stop him/her/them — that she doesn't is
  the point). Write the feeder's attentiveness, not just the food.
- **Magical/device growth:** establish rules (trigger, rate, limits,
  reversibility) and then keep them — kink readers are rules lawyers;
  a device that behaves consistently is hotter than chaos. Malfunctions
  escalate scale, never suffering.
- **Immobility:** shift the verbs from movement to logistics and devotion —
  reach, assistance, arrangements, the room reorganized around her throne.
  Give her power in stillness (she directs; the world orbits).
- **Public beats:** the audience is a mirror for HER register — the same
  double-take lands as mortification (high shame) or as tribute (owned).
  Never let the narration side with mockery; observers admire, marvel, or
  flusteredly fail to be casual.

## 5. Player consent surfaces

Ship with: a content/kink menu at game start (toggle modes on/off), heat
cap setting, and per-scene opt-in for the explicit band. Respect toggles in
content selection, not by censoring mid-scene. This is both ethics and
craft — players relax and engage deeper when the boundaries are theirs.

## 6. The taboo rail (absolute)

No minors or ambiguity about adulthood, ever. No non-consent framed as
erotic. No medical decline, death, or real suffering as content. No
narrative-voice fat-shaming (character shame is a psychology dial; the
world of the game finds her desirable). When in doubt on any of these,
don't write it — flag it.
